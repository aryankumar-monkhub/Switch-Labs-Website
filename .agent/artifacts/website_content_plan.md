### 2.5 Financing Options
**Path:** `/solutions/financing`

**Hero Section:**
- **Headline:** "Flexible Financing for Your EV Transition"
- **Subheadline:** "Multiple financial models designed to maximize ROI and minimize upfront investment."

**Financing Models:**

1. **Pay-Per-Kilometer (PPK)**
   - Zero upfront investment
   - Pay only for kilometers driven
   - All-inclusive: vehicle, charging, maintenance
   - Ideal for: Variable demand, pilot programs

2. **Monthly Subscription**
   - Fixed monthly fee
   - Unlimited kilometers (within agreed range)
   - Predictable budgeting
   - Ideal for: Steady operations, long-term planning

3. **Lease-to-Own**
   - Lower monthly payments
   - Option to purchase after lease term
   - Tax benefits on lease payments
   - Ideal for: Companies wanting eventual ownership

4. **CAPEX Purchase with O&M**
   - Outright vehicle purchase
   - Switch Labs handles operations & maintenance
   - Infrastructure as a service
   - Ideal for: Large enterprises, balance sheet optimization

**ROI Calculator:**
- Interactive tool comparing diesel vs. EV costs
